TeamOne Logistics:
Protecting Legacy. Preparing for Growth.

BACKGROUND

Founded in 2003 by Jerry Gray, TeamOne Logistics is a workforce partner based in Alpharetta, Georgia, that is focused on the asset-based transportation and logistics industry. Aiming to be an enhanced alternative to typical staffing and leasing companies, TeamOne offers expertise and solutions in recruiting, safety, compliance, and insurance. “In the Transportation logistics industry, finding and keeping safe, qualified employees is a tall task. With TeamOne logistics as your partner, that tall task is our top priority,” said CEO Page Siplon.

PARTNERSHIP

The transportation logistics industry comes with many unique issues and TeamOne needed a partner to help navigate those industry-specific issues and execute a top-line growth strategy plan. As TeamOne consider the options, Georgia Oaks stood out due to its commitment to local businesses, economic growth, and focus on securing legacy. The partnership launched in December of 2012, and Georgia Oaks assisted TeamOne on several fronts by focusing on a long-term strategy plan and providing additional leadership capabilities. Georgia Oaks operating partner Dean Ditmar joined TeamOne as Chief Commercial Officer to assist CEO Page Siplon in growing the company. Siplon spoke about the impact the partnership has had on the company: “Sharing my vision for growth with the team drives strong morale in a big way. Our growth impacts every single one of us on the team, and we all need to work together to make it happen.”

RESULTS

Today, TeamOne has become leaders in the transportation logistics industry. The COVID-19 pandemic accentuated several challenges in transportation logistics, such as increased driver shortages and costs of shipping. “Both finding people and keeping them is becoming more and more difficult,” Siplon explained. “In March, we hit a record low for commercial driver availability.” TeamOne is trusted to help its clients navigate these issues through an experienced team of employees with broad expertise across the country. A dedicated focus on building legacy and economic growth through the partnership with Georgia Oaks has helped TeamOne become a nationally recognized workforce partner.
